About BarBurGer

Barburger is all about the passion for burgers. The first open-kitchen-bar burger restaurant in Indonesia offers a varied selection of gourmet style burgers, halal hotdogs, fried chicken, fresh and hygienic food. Served in quick, warm and friendly service, the restaurant pampers fastfood lovers from sight down to the taste bud of delectable premium quality burgers, making truly burgerlovers stay young and pleasure forever.
